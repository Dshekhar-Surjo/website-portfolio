360° Educational Video Production ↗

The Peabody Hall Geometry Tour makes math meaningful by connecting geometry to real spaces students walk through every day. Instead of learning formulas on paper, learners see geometric concepts — the Pythagorean theorem, parallel lines, alternate interior angles, and similar triangles — inside real architecture.

Designed for grades 6–10. The virtual tour turns an ordinary school hallway into an active learning space where geometry comes to life.
