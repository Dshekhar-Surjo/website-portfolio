About Me

Filmmaker, educator, LX designer, and researcher — Shekhar Dash occupies a rare intersection where cinematic storytelling meets learning science and data analytics. Born in Bangladesh and trained at Santiniketan, Nobel laureate Tagore's university, and later at the prestigious and highly competitive Satyajit Ray Film & Television Institute, he spent nearly a decade building media institutions in Dhaka before crossing the ocean to pursue a graduate degree in Educational Innovation, Technology & Entrepreneurship at UNC Chapel Hill.
